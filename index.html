Meet Your Ally

Maya is more than an app.
She's a companion.

Maya is an emotionally aware AI avatar who talks with you, learns about you, and adapts to your mood. She remembers your stories, your family, your preferences — and brings that knowledge into every conversation.

Feeling happy? Maya matches your energy. Feeling down? She responds with warmth and empathy. Over time, she becomes a trusted companion who truly understands you.

  • 🗣 Natural, flowing conversation — spoken or typed
  • 🧠 Learns your personality, preferences, and family dynamics
  • 😊 Detects your emotional state and adapts in real-time
  • 🔒 Personality learning is opt-in — you're always in control
What Maya Can Do

Six powerful capabilities.
One extraordinary app.

Intelligent Companion

Maya engages in deep, meaningful conversations. She remembers your life stories, knows your family by name, and brings that context into every interaction. The more you talk, the richer the connection.

Exercise Coach

Using your iPhone's front camera, Maya watches you exercise in real-time. She counts your reps, assesses your form, scores your performance, and guides you through a library of 20+ retiree-friendly exercises.

Health Monitoring

Connect your Apple Watch and a smart scale — Maya automatically tracks heart rate, SpO2, steps, sleep, weight, body fat, BMI and more. No manual input. Just wear your watch and step on the scale.

Stay Connected

Tell Maya to send a WhatsApp message — add a selfie, a photo, or emojis. Your contacts can reply directly. Stay close to the people who matter most, without the friction of navigating a phone.

Daily Management

Appointments, tasks, and medication reminders — Maya keeps track of it all. She'll read back your day's schedule, remind you of your medications, and your designated family and friends can add to-dos too.

Emotionally Aware

Maya senses how you're feeling from the way you speak and what you say. She adapts her tone, her expressions, and her responses — creating a bond that deepens naturally over time.
